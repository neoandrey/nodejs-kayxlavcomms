The Business Concept

KayXlav Communications is committed to the consistent printing of high quality large format material as well as the precise and timely delivery of ads in the industry.In a similar vein, KayXlav strives to satisfy all customers by meeting the effective demand of agencies, corporate and middle class outdoor companies. We are dedicated to deploying appropriate technology, employing skilled and qualified personnel, ensuring a safe operating environment in addition to the effective and efficient use of resources at all time.

The vision

To meet customers' delivery specifications and schedule with extremely high quality of production and passing the messages of our clients in the most effective manner.

The Mission

To consistently produce quality world-class media that stimulate the interests of the targeted audience.

Background

Our genre of advertising is tailored towards supporting outdoor advertising and printing which has a large market considering the current population of Nigeria.
In the light of this development and to eliminate the gap in the market in regard to the rate of expansion of competitive companies/manufacturers of similar products, there is an inherent need for mass aware campaigns and a reduction in the delivery period of large format adverts. Our focus is influenced by the mutual factors of the rather few players in the market to support the huge requirements of companies involved in a 'survival of the fittest' battle leading to a penchant for avenues that aid continuous production and wealth maximization. Meanwhile, the vast number of opportunities in the country has contributed to the burgeoning number of foreign investors that have increasingly called for outdoor adverts. it is Our aim to leverage on our access to modern equipment and state-of-art machines to prepare our designs. Our area of business emphasis is the production of outdoor media such as Flexi, Mesh, Self Adhesive, Vinyl, Window graphic and so forth.
